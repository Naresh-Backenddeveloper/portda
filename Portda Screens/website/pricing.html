FAQ

Common questions

Why both subscription AND commission?

Subscription unlocks access to leads — it's what gets requests in front of you. The 5% commission applies only when a lead actually converts to a confirmed order, so PORTDA only earns when you earn. This keeps incentives aligned: we curate quality leads and you only pay extra when you close.

What counts as a "lead"?

A lead is a request that matches your service category and at least one of your served ports. Each plan caps how many leads you can view per month. Premium (high-value) leads are gated to Pro and Elite tiers.

How is the 5% commission calculated?

It's 5% of the buyer-confirmed quote amount, deducted automatically from your settlement at order completion. You'll see every deduction in the Commission Ledger inside the app.

Why does PORTDA review my quotes before sending them?

Every quote is reviewed by PORTDA for completeness, pricing sanity and policy compliance before being released to the buyer. Median review time is 18 minutes. You'll be notified once approved (and the quote goes to the buyer) or returned with feedback for edits.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes. From Plan & Billing inside the app go to Change Plan. Upgrades are prorated; downgrades take effect at next renewal. No lock-in on any tier.

Is GST charged on the subscription fee?

Yes — 18% GST applies and is shown separately on the invoice. You can claim the input credit if you're GST-registered.

Do you charge buyers anything?

No. The buyer side of PORTDA is free forever — sign up, post requests, compare quotes and pay vendors with no platform fee.

What payment methods are accepted?

UPI, all major credit/debit cards, net banking, and (for subscription auto-renew) e-NACH mandates. Yearly plans can also be paid by NEFT/RTGS with UTR confirmation.
